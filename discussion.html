Discussion Questions

  1. Do you think playing a gacha game or purchasing and opening a loot box is considered gambling? Why or why not?
  2. Should these algorithms be highly regulated, or should their design be left to developers’ and players’ personal ethics?
  3. Are lottery-based algorithms what make gacha games? Are these algorithms the most integral entertainment aspect of the games, such that they would not be as popular or enjoyable without them?
  4. Are psychological and financial impacts on consumers the responsibility of consumers and their communities, or are the developers/designers of these algorithms responsible for resulting gambling addictions?
  5. There are many regulations surrounding minors’ gambling. Should minors be permitted to play games with lottery algorithm components or should companies place age restrictions on the games entirely?
  6. Which option do you find most appealing and why?
  7. What are other options or considerations that could alleviate some of the ethical issues involved with lottery-based algorithms?
